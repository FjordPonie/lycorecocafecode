This site, simply put, is a love-letter to lycoris recoil! It's a way for us to appreciate the time and love that's been put into it, and also a way for us to show our own takes on the series and how the show affected us. The site will eventually be open for submissions for articles, fanart, and more!

We're currently working to get the site up and running, with a functional gif gallery, fanart section, and blog section with articles written by ourselves and others on Lycoris Recoil.

This site is a big work in progress and is made possible via a monthly neocities subscription! If you'd like to support us, you can donate to our Ko-Fi, or share the site with your friends! If you'd like to help out, email fiberopticpuppy@gmail.com or dm @meadowdogs on discord!
